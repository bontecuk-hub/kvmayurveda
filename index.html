200+ Years of Healing Excellence

The Kunhimon Vydair Memorial Ayurveda Centre represents over two centuries of traditional healing wisdom passed down through generations. Located in the serene landscapes of Malappuram, Kerala, we continue the legacy of authentic Ayurvedic treatment.

Our centre combines time-tested traditional methods with modern facilities to provide comprehensive healthcare solutions. From chronic pain management to postnatal care, we offer specialized treatments under expert supervision.

With qualified Ayurvedic doctors, experienced therapists, and a commitment to authentic practices, we have successfully treated thousands of patients, helping them achieve optimal health and wellness naturally.
